Orçun Yazıcı

Crack /an urban (vocational) glass and ceramic institute

_university METU, Turkey
_semester BSc in Architecture, 2nd year design studio
_year 2016
_location Kazıkiçi Bostanları, Ankara, Turkey
_author(s) Okan Alptürk, Esra Gök, Batuhan Sezer, Orçun Yazıcı


Kazıkiçi Bostanları is one of the forgotten areas that is cleaned of construction to fulfill the demands of urban transformation. An Urban (Vocational) Glass and Ceramic Institute is going to be located in the center of the area and it is expected to become a new social condenser in an urban context that could bring different layers of everyday life.
