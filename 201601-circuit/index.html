Circuit /architecture that heals

_university METU, Turkey
_semester BSc in Architecture, 3rd year design studio
_year 2016
_location Saraçoğlu Mahallesi, Ankara, Turkey
_author(s) Orçun Yazıcı


Saraçoğlu Quarter, an example of architecture in Republic Period, is in bad condition because of lack of care and misuse. Purpose of 'Circuit' project is to protect the heritage of neighbourhood both architecturally and socially while creating a space that affects itself and its whole surrounding which are called 'components'. In this manner, Saraçoğlu Quarter is going to be considered as a 'core (motherboard)' part of the city that serves as a microcosm for the whole city.

The Quarter has an unique location and identical history with full of conflicts which is still discussed. Technological scenario approach is the key part of the project and stands for an accessible and sustainable future which also preserves the site's history. To achieve educational approach for the city, proposal is designed as an extension building of Namık Kemal Primary School.
