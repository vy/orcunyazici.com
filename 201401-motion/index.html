Orçun Yazıcı

Motion

_university METU, Turkey
_semester BSc in Architecture, 1st year design studio
_year 2014
_author(s) Orçun Yazıcı


“Motion” is a tectonic structure that analyses the relation between movement, time, the body and the physical environment. It tries to capture the movements of a dancer to define a playground space. It forms a complex system that operates as a whole.
