Vision of Nature /a cultural, social and recreational niche in Antalya

_university METU, Turkey
_semester BSc in Architecture, 4th year design studio
_year 2017
_location Antalya, Turkey
_author(s) Orçun Yazıcı


Antalya is located on the southern coast of Turkey. Site is situated on the old Antalya stadium area. Main approach is preserving the urban memory of old Antalya stadium. Therefore, proposal is keeping the idea of watching from upper position like a stadium seats and preserving the open space that is created by the stadium through the silhouette of the city. Antalya is also known with its touristic characteristics, climates and the most importantly with its various art festivals. Most of the artworks are being exhibited on the streets and open to public. Design proposal is following the scenario of creating cultural, art center that offer extraordinary exhibition experiences.
