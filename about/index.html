Orçun Yazıcı

Hi, my name is Orçun and I am an architect from Ankara, Turkey. I received my bachelor's degree in architecture from Middle East Technical University (Ankara, Turkey) in 2018. Currently, I study in Ku Leuven (Ghent, Belgium) for my MSc degree with a focus on Sustainable and Resilient Architecture.
I hope as you look through this website you can get a better understanding of my works and my approach to architecture. Hopefully, you will also find some inspiration. If you would like to know more about me or my work, feel free to contact me!
